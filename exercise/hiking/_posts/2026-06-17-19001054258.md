Our entry into the Wonderland Trail, we left White River camp in the morning and hiked up to Summerland. 

The old growth forest at the bottom was awe inspiring. So quiet. And there was snow near the summit of Summerland, to cap it all off. A lively entrance to the loop.

Ashford, WA, United States (Wonderland Trail Climb)
