Is it Nickel or Nickle Creek? We see signs on the trail spelled both ways. 

This was a long day of paths crossing the road and passing people but there were some beautiful moments, especially the waterfalls. Lunch at Martha Falls was breathtakingly beautiful. 

There was a portion of the trail beside Steven’s Creek that had been hit by a landslide and we had to detour down to the riverbed for a while. The climb down and up turned a 5min section into an hour long detour. 

Paradise River really does look the part.

Packwood, WA, United States (Stevens Canyon Road Climb)
