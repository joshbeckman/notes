The sole objective today was to increase my cadence by 5% and run at 160spm ([reasoning here](https://www.joshbeckman.org/exercise/17377551610)). I found a playlist of popular songs mixed to 160bpm and listened to the inanity throughout. I was prepared to see a slower time, expecting my stride to compress, but apparently my body has a stride length constant and the faster steps just increased my speed overall by like 5%. What a trick! I don’t even feel more exhausted. 

I think the ideal 160spm song might be *Shake It Off*?
