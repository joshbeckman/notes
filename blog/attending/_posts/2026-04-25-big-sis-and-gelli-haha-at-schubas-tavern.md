I had been looking forward to seeing Gelli since [their album last summer](https://www.joshbeckman.org/blog/listening/switcheroo-by-gelli-haha) and immediately bought tickets when they announced a Chicago show.

<img width="3549" height="1996" alt="Big Sis in big shirts" src="/assets/images/75926015-667b-46c8-8a9e-851508bd28b1.jpeg" />

Unfortunately, our babysitter had to cancel last-minute and so we had to leave the concert early, only a couple songs into Gelli's performance. We got to see all of Big Sis, though, and they were a fun thrashy mash-up of [Confidence Man](https://www.joshbeckman.org/blog/attending/confidence-man-at-chicago-pride-fest) and [100 Gecs](https://www.joshbeckman.org/blog/attending/100-gecs-at-empty-bottle).
