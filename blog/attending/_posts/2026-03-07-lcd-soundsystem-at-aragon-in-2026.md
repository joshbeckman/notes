I've seen LCDS many times before ([previously](https://www.joshbeckman.org/blog/attending/lcd-soundsystem-at-aragon-ballroom)), but not as many as my friend Ned. It's a wonderful experience going to see this band with Ned. He knows how they structure their shows for different countries, he knows what the set will be, he dances more than anyone else in the crowd, and he makes me have the most fun.

![A disco ball makes every concert better](/assets/images/29162046-a8d4-4b1a-9cfa-4d24c38a3e88.jpeg)

I think _Dance Yrself Clean_ might be the best drop in all of concert music. The bass, the falsetto synth, the minimal screams. Every time it hits the whole crowd goes crazy.

{{ "https://music.apple.com/us/album/dance-yrself-clean/693387639?i=693387645" | itunesify}}

![The disco ball spins the crowd](/assets/images/b8238d45-4d9f-4449-b45a-05376a93d55d.gif)

[Automatic](https://automatic.band/) was their opener and I went halfway through their set before realizing that I had listened to their album _[Is It Now?](https://ffm.to/isitnow)_ a **lot** last year. What a lovely surprise.
