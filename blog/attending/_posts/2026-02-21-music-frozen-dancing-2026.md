Of course we went to [Music Frozen Dancing](https://www.musicfrozendancing.com/) again this year. And this time we got a spot near the front so we could get our faces visible in the annual photo. A fun bit from [last year](https://www.joshbeckman.org/blog/attending/music-frozen-dancing-2025): I'm kinda featured in the homepage video loop (I'm the guy in the bright yellow hat fighting through the crowd to hug his friends).

![Blustery snow was falling for the last two acts of the festival](/assets/images/c2781a7a-51f1-4832-a643-f5d9e29a3356.jpeg)

## Body Shop

> This is a chord. This is another. This is a third. Now form a band.
> - Tony Moon

I always think of this concept at the first band of this festival. Minimum punk.

## Snuffed

I don't know how the screamo singers can keep going for a hour.

## Good Flying Birds

This one slid off me after they were done. Standard jangly rock.

{{ "https://music.apple.com/us/album/down-on-me/1817694381?i=1817694383" | itunesify }}

## Lip Critic

I had seen them open for [Fat Dog at The Empty Bottle](https://www.joshbeckman.org/blog/attending/fat-dog-at-the-empty-bottle) a year ago and they were even better this time around. Just good heavy synth hard rock with double-drummers.

{{ "https://music.apple.com/us/album/milky-max/1783172639?i=1783172986" | itunesify }}

## Los Thuthanaka

The final act was interesting for a bit, but there's only so long that I can enjoy drone-noise-dj-loops while standing in the snowfall. Also they ended early, which was weird.
