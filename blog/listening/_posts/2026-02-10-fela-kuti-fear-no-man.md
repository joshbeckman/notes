The [_Fela Kuti: Fear No Man_ podcast](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/fela-kuti-fear-no-man/id1835735529) from Jad Abumrad was a beautiful series I listened to while [shoveling snow](https://www.joshbeckman.org/exercise/16612881848) and [lifting](https://www.joshbeckman.org/exercise/16898640179) [weights](https://www.joshbeckman.org/exercise/16950132660) this winter. I had been listening to Fela's music for a decade but never had I delved into his history and impact. Now I hear more in every song.
