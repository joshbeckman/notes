<p>I’ve been listening to this album when lifting weights at the gym recently. The <em>Justice</em> bass pushes me so hard I find myself dancing on the bench.</p><p>This is the first album of hers that I’ve listened to, but I didn’t expect to see that it’s actually her fourth. It plays to me like a first album: lots of reference sounds (the <em>Justice</em> electro, the <em>Charli XCX</em> lyrics), mixture of genres and tempos. Maybe that just her brand and it’s established and I’m just the one learning it.</p><p>I’m excited to see her play at Lollapalooza this year.</p>
