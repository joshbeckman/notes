![TON UP by Gold Panda](https://cdn.rcrd.club/releases/znp5kgev4g3lvx3q.jpg)

<p>This new work from Gold Panda sounds like he spent the winter with Daphni and came out convinced of the power of the bop and the beat and the bass. I love this to start my summer.</p><p>Some of it gets a bit repetitive - he hasn’t fully fleshed out a range of melodies to pull from - but the grooves are there and I love the mid-album piano riff tune that reminds you of Gold Panda’s gentler roots.</p>
