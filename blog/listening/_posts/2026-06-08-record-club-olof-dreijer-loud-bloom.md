<p>I started listening to this without any context and thought: this sounds like a little bit of <em>The Knife </em>and a little bit of <em>Floating Points</em>. And lo and behold, it is! At least the first half of my estimation. It sounds like he’s using literally the same synths and sound board.</p><p>I really like the first half of the album but the second half fades out for me, until the ending swoop of fun.</p>
