<p>I'm getting a workbench delivered to me today.</p>

<p>I've built these big shelves on the wall of my studio/office/workshop this year, but I've come to realize that shelves are not a workable surface.</p>

<p>They are too close to the wall. When clamping a piece of wood to them, you only get one - maybe two - corners of leverage. When you hack at something on a shelf, the wall shakes. Things fall on your head. When working with a piece on a working surface, you need to get on all sides of it. You can't do that with a wall in your way.</p>

<p>So I'm going to be taking down my big low shelves and using a workmate instead.</p>
