I picked up Printer's Row Coffee Co.'s [Brazil Cerrado](https://www.printersrowcoffeeco.com/shop/brazil-cerrado-12oz) only a couple days after it was roasted.
We walked over as a family on Saturday morning and the roastery was *busy* with other families and couples after a run. The table beside us was writing either a TikTok skit or a screenplay, I couldn't divine which.

![Bag of Brazil Cerrado from Printer's Row Coffee Co.](/assets/images/L1000405.jpeg)

| Aspects | |
|---------|-|
| Roast | Medium |
| Origin | Brazil (Cerrado) |
| Process | Fully washed & sun dried |
| Roast Date | 2026-04-15 |

In the smell, I find sweet walnut and chocolate, really like a candy coffee.

| Brew | |
|---------|-|
| Grind | Medium-Fine |
| Method | Chemex pour-over |
| Ratio | 1:14 |
| Water Temp | 199&deg;F |

This smooth brew tastes immediately like baker's chocolate and mandarin orange. With such a deep flavor, it still tastes crip, wafting away off my tongue.
I really like this one.
