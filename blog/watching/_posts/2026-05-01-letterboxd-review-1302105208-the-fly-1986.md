He glues together an already tight script with his pent-up energy, continuously twisting the knot in my stomach until I realized that the knot was going to remain past the third act. This is man-vs-self in one of its best forms, allowing everyone to see part of themselves in his downfall: the terror of your own body, the deep despair of living with mistakes.</p><p>Matt and Brandon and I are trying to eat food related to the movie on our horror nights.
