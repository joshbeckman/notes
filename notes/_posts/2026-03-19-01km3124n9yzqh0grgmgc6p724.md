This is your reminder that body cams are not police reform. They are a taxpayer expense to show the pretense of accountability while allowing easy avoidance by those supposedly monitored, while actually adding to the surveillance of regular citizens. 

Real police reform reforms the police to be less militaristic and to have less power.
