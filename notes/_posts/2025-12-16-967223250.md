> [Weasel words](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Weasel_word) are words and phrases aimed at creating an impression that something specific and meaningful has been said, when in fact only a vague or ambiguous claim has been communicated. A common form of weasel wording is through vague attribution, where a statement is [dressed with authority](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Argument_from_authority), yet has no substantial basis.
