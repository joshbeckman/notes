> It’s worth remembering that the CTO’s primary role is to [work with exec peers across the business](https://www.annashipman.co.uk/jfdi/people-process-tech-business.html) to deliver business outcomes, and that is the same for all of the exec team, and the CEO.
> 
> So any proposal you make has to make it clear that this is about the business, not about improving engineering for its own purposes.
