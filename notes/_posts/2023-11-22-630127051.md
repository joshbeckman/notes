The Mingei philosophy of "nameless craftsmen" building beautiful everyday objects from local materials - see the [Folk Interfaces note](https://www.joshbeckman.org/notes/472420699) - is related: wonder doesn't require scale, it requires ingenuity within reach, which is also why I built [My Studio Stool](https://www.joshbeckman.org/blog/practicing/my-studio-stool) slightly off-pattern and still ended up with something more mine than the blueprint.
